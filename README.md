 # Plastic waste ingested by six fish species in the Central Brazilian Amazon

This repository contains the data and R scripts used for the study on plastic debris ingestion by commercial fish species in the region of Tefé, Amazonas State, Brazil.



## Abstract

The study investigates the occurrence, shape, color, and chemical composition (via FTIR) of plastic debris in the gastrointestinal tracts of 336 specimens across six fish species heavily consumed by the local population in the heart of the Amazon:

*Semaprochilodus insignis*, *Osteoglossum bicirrhosum*, *Cichla monoculus*, *Colossoma macropomum*, *Triportheus elongatus*, and *Mylossoma albiscopum*



## data 

Data files containing fish metrics, ingestion counts, and FTIR spectral bands found.
